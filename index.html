About Ramit Sethi.

Ramit Singh Sethi is an American personal finance advisor and entrepreneur. Sethi is the author of the 2009 New York Times Best Seller, I Will Teach You to Be Rich and founder of GrowthLab.com, owner of IWillTachYouToBeRich.com and owner and a co-founder of PBworks, a commercial wiki website.

Ramit Sethi's Goal

I believe Ramit's goal is to help everyone understand finance so you can live your rich life. He does this by sharing his knowledge with everyone through his personal finance book "I Will Teach You To Be Rich" and programs.
